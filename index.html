Project Oort+

"The future doesn’t belong to those who wait. It belongs to those who dare to go further." – Tyler Jones

Mission

To send humanity’s deepest message and explore the outermost edge of our solar system—the Oort Cloud.

Voyager 3 will be equipped with AI navigation, fusion propulsion, and humanity’s cultural archive onboard.

Vision

Unite science, art, and culture in a space mission that outlives generations and inspires the world.
